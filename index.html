Financial Formulas

Net Present Value (NPV):

$$ \text{NPV} = \sum_{t=0}^{T} \frac{C_t}{(1+r)^t} $$

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR):

$$ \text{CAGR} = \left(\frac{V_{end}}{V_{start}}\right)^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1 $$
