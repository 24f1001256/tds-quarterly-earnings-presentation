Financial Formulae

Net Interest Margin (NIM):

\(\mathrm{NIM} = \dfrac{\text{Interest Income} - \text{Interest Expense}}{\text{Average Earning Assets}} \times 100\)

Return on Equity (ROE):

\(\mathrm{ROE} = \dfrac{\text{Net Income}}{\text{Average Shareholders' Equity}} \times 100\)
